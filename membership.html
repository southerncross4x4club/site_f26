Our club intentionally stays small to maintain strong connections between members and ensure quality experiences on every trip. Here's everything you need to know about joining.

How It Works

Joining Process

Our membership process helps us find people who share our values and will contribute positively to the club culture. Here's how it works.

1

Nomination

A prospective member must be nominated by a current member who knows them and believes they'll be a good fit for the club.

2

Trial Period

Prospects attend 2–3 club events to experience our culture, get a feel for how we operate, and meet current members.

3

Formal Proposal

After the trial period, the nominating member formally proposes the prospect for membership. A second member must support the proposal.

4

Member Vote

The club votes by anonymous ballot. A prospect needs 70% approval from the membership to be accepted.

5

Concerns Process

If any member has serious reservations about a prospect, they can speak confidentially with the committee before the vote takes place.

What We Look For

Member Criteria

To become a member, prospects must demonstrate the following.

Demonstrates good judgement in assessing track conditions and their own capabilities
Supports and welcomes new members, sharing knowledge and experience generously
Represents the club positively when interacting with landowners, other user groups, and the general public
Is respectful in their dealings with other club members
Participates constructively in club activities, meetings, and decision-making
Acts honestly and with integrity in all club-related matters
Ready to Join?

Member Application
