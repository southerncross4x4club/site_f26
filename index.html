What We Stand For

Our Values

🏕️

Here for Adventure

We're here to get out there — camping trips, 4WD tracks, and time spent in the great outdoors. If you love the bush and live for the next adventure, you'll fit right in.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family friendly

Whether you're looking for a fun trip out with the family, or a tough day on the tracks, we offer a safe, respectful crew with something for everyone. Santa has even been known to pay a visit to our Christmas party.

🌿

Respect the Bush

We look after each other and the places we explore. Stick to legal tracks, leave no trace, and treat your fellow travellers right — on and off the trail.

Get Involved

Join the Club

Our club intentionally stays small to maintain strong connections between members and ensure quality experiences on every trip. Our membership process helps us find people who share our values and will contribute positively to the club culture.

We're always open to welcoming new members — but places are capped, so when a spot becomes available we take the time to find the right fit. New members join as prospects first, attending 3–4 events to get a feel for the club and get to know our members before their application is formally reviewed.

If that sounds like something you're up for, we'd love to hear from you.
