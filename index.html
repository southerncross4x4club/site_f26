What We Stand For

Our Values

🤝

Mateship

We look after each other on and off the track. No one gets left behind — whether you're bogged, broken down, or just need a hand with camp setup.

🌿

Tread Lightly

We respect the land we explore. We stick to legal tracks, pack out our rubbish, and leave campsites better than we found them.

🛡️

Safety First

Every run is planned with safety as the priority. We carry recovery gear, communicate clearly, and ensure all drivers are ready for the terrain ahead.

🧭

Adventure for All

From beginners to seasoned veterans, we welcome all experience levels. We run trips that cater to everyone and help newer drivers build their skills.

Get Involved

Join the Club

Our club intentionally stays small to maintain strong connections between members and ensure quality experiences on every trip. Our membership process helps us find people who share our values and will contribute positively to the club culture.

We're always open to welcoming new members — but places are capped, so when a spot becomes available we take the time to find the right fit. New members join as prospects first, attending 3–4 events to get a feel for the club and get to know our members before their application is formally reviewed.

If that sounds like something you're up for, we'd love to hear from you.
